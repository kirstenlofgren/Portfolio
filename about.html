Hello!

I'm Kirsten, a UX/UI Designer and artist originally from Kingsport, Tennessee. I studied Art and Education at Brigham Young University with concentrations in Photography, Oil Painting, and K-12 Education. After graduating, I became an artist and early grades art teacher working in freelance graphic design on the side. These days, I use my background in developmental psychology, art, and design to create inclusive designs as a UX Designer. I live, work and design in Austin, TX.
